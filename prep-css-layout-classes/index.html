Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Indiana Jones basically steals a historical artifact which he shouldn't have and the Nazis come after him because they want the power of said artifact to take over the world. Lost of action of people trying to take the artifact all while trying to win over female character. The Nazis basically end up opening it and dying from the artifact's power. Indiana Jones takes aka steals the artifact back to the US.

Temple of Doom

Indiana Jones ends up sticking his nose in someone else's business of children slavery and kidnapping in India. There's a token asian kid for comedic relief and representation possibly. Also a token female character Indiana tries to woo over. As if that weren't enough, there's another token dangerous booby trap lined location. Tries to be a good citizen to put an end to evil campaign, rescue lost children, win the girl and conquer the dangerous Temple.

The Last Crusade

Indiana Jones looks for another historical artifact, but is moreso intrigued by how his own father was searching for it and never returned. Nazis once again look for the same historical artifact. Some action scenes to see who gets the artifact first. Some people die. Indiana and father survives.
